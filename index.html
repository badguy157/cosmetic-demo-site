Frequently asked questions

When performed by a qualified medical doctor, these treatments have excellent safety profiles. We use only approved products, follow strict protocols, and manage any complications effectively. All potential risks are discussed before treatment.

Most patients report minimal discomfort. It is often described as a slight pinch or pressure. We use fine needles and can apply numbing cream if needed. Many are surprised at how quick and comfortable the process is.

Most treatments have minimal to no downtime. You might have slight redness or swelling for a few hours, rarely a small bruise. Most patients return to normal activities right away. Some even book treatments during lunch breaks.

Pricing depends on your specific treatment plan. A consultation is $150, anti-wrinkle treatments start from $250 per area, and fillers vary by volume needed. We provide exact costs upfront. There are no hidden fees or surprise charges.

Anti-wrinkle treatments naturally fade over 3-4 months. Fillers can be dissolved if needed, though this is rarely necessary with our conservative approach. We work gradually to ensure you're happy with each step.
