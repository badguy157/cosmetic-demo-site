Frequently asked questions

How long do treatments take?

Most treatments take between 15-45 minutes depending on the areas being treated. We'll give you a clear timeframe during your consultation.

Is there downtime?

Most treatments have minimal to no downtime. Some may have mild temporary side effects like redness or swelling. We'll discuss what to expect for your specific treatment.

Who will carry out my treatment?

All treatments are performed by Dr. Emily Hart, a qualified medical doctor with specialist training in aesthetic medicine.

Do I need a consultation first?

Yes, we require an initial consultation for all new patients. This allows us to assess your needs, discuss your goals, and create a personalized treatment plan.

How long do results last?

Results vary depending on the treatment type and individual factors. Anti-wrinkle treatments typically last 3-4 months, while dermal fillers can last 6-18 months. We'll discuss expected duration during consultation.

Are treatments safe?

When performed by qualified medical professionals, these treatments have excellent safety profiles. We use only approved products and follow strict safety protocols. All potential risks will be discussed before treatment.
