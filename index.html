Frequently asked questions

Will I look fake or overdone?

No. Our approach is conservative and tailored to your features. We aim for subtle enhancement that keeps you looking like yourself—refreshed, not "done." You can always add more, but you can't easily undo an excessive result.

Are treatments safe?

When performed by a qualified medical doctor, these treatments have excellent safety profiles. We use only approved products, follow strict protocols, and manage any complications effectively. All potential risks are discussed before treatment.

How much does it hurt?

Most patients report minimal discomfort—often described as a slight pinch or pressure. We use fine needles and can apply numbing cream if needed. Many are surprised at how quick and comfortable the process is.

Is there downtime afterward?

Most treatments have minimal to no downtime. You might have slight redness or swelling for a few hours, rarely a small bruise. Most patients return to normal activities immediately—some even book treatments during lunch breaks.

How much will it actually cost?

Pricing depends on your specific treatment plan. A consultation is $150, anti-wrinkle treatments start from $250 per area, and fillers vary by volume needed. We provide exact costs upfront—no hidden fees or surprise charges.

What if I don't like the results?

Anti-wrinkle treatments naturally fade over 3-4 months. Fillers can be dissolved if needed, though this is rarely necessary with our conservative approach. We work gradually to ensure you're happy with each step.
